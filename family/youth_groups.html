I9 Sports

The nation's largest multi-sport provider focused solely on high-quality, community-based youth sports programs, i9 Sports® is a popular league for all ages and skill levels throughout the Durham / Chapel Hill area. We focus on making things fun for kids, providing age-appropriate instruction for all players, and ensuring our programs are convenient for busy families.

North Carolina FC Youth

NCFC Youth Recreation provides an inclusive, community-focused soccer program for kids ages 2½ to 18. With weekly practices and weekend games led by volunteer coaches, it promotes teamwork, skill development, and a love for the game. Serving Chapel Hill and surrounding areas, it's one of the largest volunteer-based youth soccer programs in the country, offering both fall and spring seasons.

Rainbow Soccer

Rainbow Soccer is a long-standing recreational soccer organization in Chapel Hill, offering inclusive programs for youth and adults of all skill levels. Established in 1972, it emphasizes fun, sportsmanship, and skill development over competition, providing a welcoming environment for players aged U4 through adulthood.
