Community Empowerment Fund (CEF)

An organization dedicated to supporting individuals experiencing homelessness or housing instability in Chapel Hill. CEF provides resources like financial coaching, housing support, and career development to empower community members.

Freedom House Recovery Center

Mental health and substance use treatment including psychiatric services and medication-assisted treatment (MAT). Freedom House also operates a Facility-Based Crisis that is accessible 24/7/365 for those experiencing a mental health crisis or who are in need of immediate substance abuse treatment (detox and stabilization).

Phone Number: 919-942-2803

UNC Alcohol & Substance Abuse Program (ASAP)

A confidential resource offering support, counseling, and treatment for students dealing with alcohol and substance use. UNC ASAP provides personalized care to help students navigate recovery and maintain well-being.

Phone Number: 984-974-6320
