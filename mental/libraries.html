Chapel Hill Public Library

Offering a wide range of books, resources, and programs for all ages. Whether you're looking for quiet study space, family-friendly events, or digital resources, the Chapel Hill Public Library has something for everyone.

Davis Library

UNC-Chapel Hill's main library, offers extensive academic resources, quiet study areas, and collaborative spaces. Eight floors tall, Davis Library is an essential space for deep dives into coursework and projects.

Wilson Library

A historic library at UNC-Chapel Hill, known for its stunning architecture and vast collection of special archives. A quiet and inspiring space for research, study, and exploration of unique collections.
