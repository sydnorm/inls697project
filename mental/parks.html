Cedar Falls Park

A peaceful spot in Chapel Hill, offering scenic walking trails, picnic areas, and beautiful views of the surrounding woods and creek.

Chapel Hill Community Center Park

This park offers a variety of programs, including fitness classes, recreational activities, and community events. With facilities for sports, arts, and meetings, it's a hub for residents to connect, learn, and stay active.

Hargraves Community Park

A vibrant park in the heart of Chapel Hill offering a variety of outdoor activities. With sports fields, playgrounds, picnic areas, and a community center, Hargraves is a great spot for families.

Southern Community Park

A spacious, family-friendly park offering playgrounds, sports fields, and scenic walking trails. With ample green space for picnics and recreational activities.
