TABLE

TABLE delivers healthy food and nutrition education to children in Orange County. They envision a community where all children have equitable access to nutritious food and knowledge to experience optimal health and dignity.

Meals on Wheels Orange County

This program delivers meals to senior citizens and others who are homebound. Volunteers assist with packing, delivery, and wellness check-ins.

PORCH Chapel Hill-Carrboro

PORCH is a grassroots hunger relief organization that collects and delivers food to local pantries. Volunteer opportunities include food sorting and delivery assistance.

Inter-Faith Council (IFC)

IFC provides meals through their Community Kitchen and groceries through their Community Market. They also welcome volunteers to support these efforts.
