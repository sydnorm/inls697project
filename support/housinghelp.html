Orange County Emergency Housing Assistance

This program offers immediate financial support to individuals and families facing housing instability. Assistance covers rent, utilities, and other urgent housing-related needs.

Ronald McDonald House of Chapel Hill

The Ronald McDonald House provides a home-away-from-home for families with seriously ill children receiving care. The House offers meals, lodging, and emotional support during hospital stays.
