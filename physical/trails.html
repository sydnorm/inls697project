Bolin Creek Trail

A popular greenway with a smooth, paved path that winds along Bolin Creek. Stretching 2.5 total miles, this scenic route is perfect for runners and is easy for pedestrians and cyclists to travel on.

Carolina North Forest

Enjoy a visit to 750 acres of woodlands located on the University of North Carolina's Carolina North campus, nestled between the towns of Chapel Hill and Carrboro. This expansive network offers a mix of gravel and dirt paths, perfect for trail running.

Morgan Creek Trail Trailhead

A peaceful, scenic trail perfect for runners of all levels. An 0.85 miles with 10' wide paved woodland trail and one steep slope at a boardwalk section, the trail is great for a quiet jog or a nature-filled run.
