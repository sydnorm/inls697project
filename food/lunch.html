Sutton's Drug Store

A nostalgic Chapel Hill gem, serving classic diner fare with a focus on breakfast and comfort food. Known for its friendly service, signature milkshakes, and old-fashioned soda fountain, Sutton’s is a must-visit for a taste of local history and delicious meals.

Merritt's Grill

An iconic destination in Chapel Hill, renowned for its mouthwatering sandwiches. With a casual, laid-back vibe, Merritt’s Grill offers flavorful meals perfect for a quick lunch.

Alpaca Peruvian Charcoal Chicken

A vibrant spot offering delicious Peruvian-style rotisserie chicken, seasoned with bold spices and cooked to perfection. Known for its smoky flavor, tender meat, and colorful sides, Alpaca is a must-try.
