Sunrise Biscuit Kitchen

A Chapel Hill favorite, serving up delicious, made-to-order biscuits with a variety of fillings, from classic sausage and egg to unique specialties. Perfect for a hearty breakfast.

Carolina Coffee Shop

A charming, historic spot in the heart of Chapel Hill offering freshly brewed coffee, delicious breakfast dishes, and Southern-inspired comfort food. Known for its cozy atmosphere, it's the perfect place to relax, study, or enjoy a meal with friends.

Crossroads Chapel Hill

A modern American restaurant within the Carolina Inn offering a diverse menu with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. With delicious brunch options, Crossroads provides a welcoming atmosphere perfect for both casual meals and special occasions.
